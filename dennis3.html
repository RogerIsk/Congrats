How to Stay humble

Learn from the worst, March 22, 2024

Honestly, this has been the easiest part. Knowing so many renowned and world class artists/DJs really puts your own ego in check, sometimes a bit too much. I'm always surrounded by people who are famous but humble, and the Coppi DJ sessions every Thursday really help me see this all the time. Never take anything or anyone for granted, show genuine interest and just do what you love, the rest will come to you naturally.

However, this is easier said than done. There's a fine line between being proud and being a douche and it's easy to cross. Should you keep your achievements to yourself because you're worried about coming off as a show-off? Or should you share them with the world because you're proud of what you've accomplished? Honestly, the only person who can answer that is you. We all suffer from imposter syndrome, I'm always the worst at it. I assume everything is common sense and knowledge, until I meet many people and see that it's not. It's hard to break out of that mindset, but it's possible. It's all about finding the right balance between being proud and being humble.
